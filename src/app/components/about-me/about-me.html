About Me

My Journey

My journey in technology began at the Federal Institute of Rio Grande do Norte (IFRN), where I worked as a teacher assistant in Object-Oriented Programming (OOP). In this role, I supported my classmates by helping them debug their code and guiding them through complex concepts whenever they had questions. This experience strengthened both my technical foundation and my ability to communicate ideas clearly.

After that, I joined Dore Refrigerantes as a junior apprentice. During this period, I developed internal applications tailored to the needs of employees, helping to improve workflows and efficiency. I was also responsible for maintaining the company’s website, ensuring it remained functional and up to date.

Currently, I work as a Full Stack Developer at Provider Global. I contribute to the development of a platform designed to assist institutions in managing purchase requests, with a focus on preventing fraud. The system includes advanced features such as AI-powered assistants and other tools aimed at improving accuracy and decision-making.

Throughout my journey, I have been driven by curiosity, problem-solving, and a constant desire to learn and build meaningful solutions.
